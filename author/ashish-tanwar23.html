Feeling guilty : Unkle_comics

This is a weird time in unkle comics. Guilt seems to be the primary emotion.

gAmit : Guilty of making promises and delivering a lot of them

gVeronica : Accepting an offer of lifetime lurv - togetherness : good things.

It is quite intense when weekend fun strats planning good times, turn into a lot of 'doubts'.

gAmit : convinced in his mind that gVeronica is going to leave him

  • sooner or later.

The ego and mind

  • doing everything in its power to hold on to the happiness that is gVeronica in his life.

gVeronica : is different now.

She seeks different.

Alkuhul doesnt seem kool anymore.

Parties seem to tire her.

Her priorities are different.

No one knows - trends/relationships/fashion...they all change.

gAmit and gVeronica talk, but lot is unsaid.

The gPhone password is known to gAmit, yet the phone seems his biggest enemy.

Whenever gVeronica takes it away, demons appear in his mind

  • separation, sigmaness, loneliness:

fear.

gVeronica senses it...she tries her best to assure him.

yet doubts remain.

it is like an unspoken word

  • a name which can be spoken, but should not be.

gAmit is nostalgic of trips and all the vows which they made to each other....families and future.

subtly reminding her of the 'words' she said and what they mean - religion is holy.

Yet, it seems 'words'.

Eyes - giving away the fear...

That is why, gAmit and gVeronica are made for each other.

They both lurved..

maybe kind of lied to themselves of the reasons..

maybe gAmit planned this lurv

...based on how it would look - money society etx

and gVeronica did the same .

..it looked good.. and it is...it was.

Maybe this is it. a happy ever after.

but doesnt feel like it...

does it?
