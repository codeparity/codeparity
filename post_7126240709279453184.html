Knowing your 0 is very important

Ashish Tanwar Paw | 03 Nov 2023

Knowing your 0 is very important

I call it grounding

Emotional 0

Material 0

Assumptions on these levels - cost dearly

I made these assumptions multiple time - multiple cracks and failures

Most difficult to debug.

But then again, this phase of my life was built of 'ugly' but useful.

My 0 really went into -ve.

But then, it was the true 0

No mentors

we will see?
