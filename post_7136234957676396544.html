Things to preserve - code of codeparity - preserving "good" things - unquestionable good

The delhi i grew up had a weird culture - every sunday, every unkle would take out his car, scooter etc - to give it a weekly routine

it was socializing, maintenance, sunbathing, exchange of tools - knowledge

  • everything logical - there was not internet then

it created an ecosystem in itself - tools, knowledge, innovation was often done in these 'money minded' yet social indian bunch - the good of capitalism

same can be said about week day/end markets - they are not just bargain spree now - money and food affordability has been been organically maintained due to culture and practicality of it

all that blah - just to underline a basic perspective of luxury vs dependence

a neighborhood where no one knows how to jack a car when its really needed

vs

a an aggregator app which provides few hours of operation, but gives a plastic shiny card?

  • the base principal - own what you buy.

hackability of it - means relevance of it - means long term usability of it.
