Layers

CS concepts have a beauty about them - they clarify the constructs of the world in a very logical, precise way.

One of the most enlightening concept is OSI model - layering of responsibilities for "compartmentalization" of work/concepts/knowledge.

A better analogy would be a multi storey building / apartment complex.

Ground floor - has some peculiar functions

  • the building stands on it

  • in/out of all "traffic"

  • all the above "floors/facilities" literally stand on it.

In CS, this concept is called OSI model

  • the lower layers are more busy

  • do "mechanical/protocoled" procedures

  • things go more "imaginative/rewarding" as you go up - presentation layers

  • new ideas

The same construct is represented in the "hierarchy of power" - the popular Illuminati triangle and ll

the prophet, does bring this point .... though in a more subtle way.

"Horizontal vs Vertical"

Layers are needed - to find a "purpose"

Without a purpose, a passion - a will, a focus with nothing or everything to 'focus' on

  • idling cpu, except it seeks work - the hack of our minds..

it is like an open port with no input filtration...tv/gossip/news/twatter/kinkedin/feels of veronica/needs of betty...

they all become a 'purpose'.
