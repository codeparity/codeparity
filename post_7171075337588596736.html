Purpose

It is not just 1 and 1s decisions.

Purpose persists in different forms.

Sometimes it is buildings

sometimes it is ideas

most of the times - it is just a memory - a stack page.

Humans may have a purpose...

But buildings definitely do - intent of a human, left behind in a creation - a material 3d 1, build to last longer than the creator.

For all 3d purposes - the human who created this 'intent' of a building, and ends up materializing it - is god of that creation - the building.

He knows the purpose - it is hidden and written all over the design, the map, the color and beauty of it.

  • a god of the building. an intent created to last in a building.

lets 'assume' this gestalt of buildings and gods and apply it to different buildings and 3d gods.
