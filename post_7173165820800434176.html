Knowledge Vs Wisdom

Knowledge is in books

an author's perspective 'understood' by the reader.

An investment of time, which leads to an invisible 'approval' or disapproval of an creator/authors' perspective (gestalt)

The gestalt which moulds in any 'product'

An author creates words

An engineer creates buildings/boxes

...

The invisible 'intent' of the creator is somehow left...

it is not just the $...the building, the followers...

something which remains in 'sub-conscious' - the cumulative 1....

a part of which can only be described as a 'state' change in matrix - $, people, moods, progress

  • time makes sure of it...

a part of something abstract - individual as well as habitus - karma.

This karma, when amplified like a wave in resonance - becomes 'truth' - the knowledge, the intent and hence 'actions'.

Wisom is something else...

it doesnt require thinking...

is seems lame - regular...

birds, dogs animals - they all do it...

some ppl call it 'flow state'...

i just call it being 'focussed' on a task.

this is not wisdom...

It is not 'progress'...

knowledge, progress and wisdom...

the 3 words which cant seem to balance themselves out...

They are not meant to be balanced?
