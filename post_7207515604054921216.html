I am quite fascinated by HCI - human computer interface...

Ashish Tanwar Paw | 15 Jun 2024

I am quite fascinated by HCI - human computer interface...

despite multiple tech leaps...

the keyboard is still reliable - classy - needed...

even by next gen...

gaming - is cutting edge in HCI

the joystick - evolving...

still - the basics : timeless

street fighter modern controls...

are quite a challenge from engineering perspective..

but none can deny the 'need' of it..

people - want 'wins' - rush of likeys..

is too 'instant' for a video game - as hard to master as Fighting Games genre.

the skill - being 'timing' and 'strategy' at large..

anticipation, reaction - the killer instinct...

all unleashed in a modern numbed society - an outlet of sorts.

yet, the modern controls - do make a great take on it...

yet, i am yet to see any modern control user - reaching top 8...

there is a damage-- when choosing 'convenience'

  • which becomes too taxing

  • when competing at top levels..

hence the 'dichotomy' of the plan ...the strategy..

is esports and this easy 'inlet' of modern controls.

..is too much of a dream?

years - thats how much it takes...even for consistency...

then - there are just few 1s who are hard to ignore..

sonicfox - legend at 16...and legend now..
