Status check - 17 May 2022

Codeparity Aka Ashish Tanwar | 17 May 2022 | Updated on 17 May 2022

Wanted to write this post - started to get a lot of check-ins from people wondering what I have been upto. Most are curious about my mentally health and wellbeing….having chosen to own my reclusive nature.

Professionally, I am on schedule. Repeating past mistakes is a pattern of insanity, quite aware of the usual. Some things become apparent as you age, for me these have been my priorities in the last 4 months.

Getting my strength back

Physical fitness is an absolute necessity in today’s environment. Having a family is a duty, and depending on law enforcement for their safety is absolute stupidity. Blessed to have the best Chief Minister of the country - Yogi Adityanath - that should not become a reason to become complacent. Law, Police in India are the most pathetic administrative public services in the world…something which won't change in the current scheme of things. Would reason in a separate post. Hand is better than before now, and the mind/body balance has been restored, though a bigger challenge of creating a sustainable routine remains.

Extending my reach

One’s opportunity and success are highly influenced by the people around them. Being born in the middle class, I am very much affected by this bubble or sphere of influence. The last time I launched Codeparity, I counted on the goodwill of the people around me - which did not work out at all. I ended up wasting weeks. Won't happen this time.

Realigning, priorities their order

I left Google in weird circumstances. No matter how I want to ignore this …. One needs time to counter for self bias and get a hold on your reality. Time is invaluable in this exercise. Being an engineer at heart, execution comes easy to me, and that has not really helped things. With all that done, I am back in the professional sphere again - not as a jobber but as an entrepreneur and investor.

See you out there. Please follow me on twitter : ashishtanwar, the wokeness of platforms like linkedin and twitter sickens me, and this is one of the few social/tech issues i would work on in coming months.

Thanks for reading!!
